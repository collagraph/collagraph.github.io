About us

Collagraph is a digital design partnership founded by Robert Irish and Luke Bacon. We design and build websites that make an impact.

Good design is focused. We help clients define and zero-in on their specific challenges and opportunities, then build websites with distinctive design and solid infrastructure that put them in control of their online presence.

Our design communicates your message to the people who matter, so that the value you offer is clear.

We’re passionate about contemporary best-practices, precise usability and open source technology so that you don’t have to be. We put clients in-front of their audience’s changing needs, optimising content for access from phones, tablets and desktops in an evermore networked, social web.
