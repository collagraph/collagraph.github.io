About us

Collagraph is a digital design partnership founded by Robert Irish and Luke Bacon.

We design and build websites, develop communications strategies, and help clients publish great content.

We’ve worked on successful projects with forward-thinking companies and organisations including the Australian Centre for Independent Journalism, Jobs Australia, the Australian National University, Sustainable House and the Australian Council of Social Services.

We’re always excited to take on new challenges, big and small. See some of our work and get in contact about your next project.
