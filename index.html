About us

Collagraph is a digital design partnership founded by Robert Irish and Luke Bacon.

We design and build websites, develop communications strategy, workshop digital content and help establish and improve workflows for producing it.

We’ve worked with The Australian Centre for Independent Journalism, Jobs Australia, The Australian Council of Social Service, Sustainable House, Goddard & Co Solicitors, Tilt Vision and ANU’s Seven Sisters Songline Project.

We’re always excited to take on new challenges, big and small. See some of our work and get in contact and tell us about your next project.
