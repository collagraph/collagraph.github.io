Christopher L. H. Cooper

Illustrator & designer Christopher L. H. Cooper approached us to help rethink his approach to presenting work on the web.

One of the key challenges of this project was to serve high-quality images quickly to users on all types of devices. We delivered a responsive website that conveys Chris's character and displays his work in a clean layout inspired by the strong grids found in broadsheet newspapers.

Northbridge Progress Association

The NPA informs and represents local residents of Northbridge, NSW. We helped them redefine the way they deliver information to suit the shifting requirements of their community.

We delivered a responsive website, a full rebranding and a tailored publishing system for local news, community events, resources on focus issues, their print newspaper and their digital newsletter.

Principles for Independent Archives

Principles for Independent Archives is an open-source project we established to share and discuss thoughts on how to build digital archives for the web.

The project is a cross-disciplinary forum for exploring the possibilities of small archive projects on the web. The project has draw contributions from professional archivist, artists, journalists and web developers thus far.
